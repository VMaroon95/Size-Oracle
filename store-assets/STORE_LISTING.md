## Name
Size Oracle — Universal Size Advisor

## Summary (132 chars max)
Privacy-first size advisor for 100+ stores. Enter your measurements once, get accurate size recommendations everywhere. 100% local.

## Description
Stop guessing your size when shopping online. Size Oracle recommends your perfect clothing and shoe size on 100+ popular stores — from Nike and Zara to Amazon and Gucci.

🔒 PRIVACY FIRST
All your measurements are stored locally on your device. Nothing is ever sent to any server. Zero tracking. Zero data collection.

🛍️ 100+ SUPPORTED STORES
Works automatically on Nike, Adidas, Zara, H&M, ASOS, Amazon, Nordstrom, Levi's, Gucci, Uniqlo, Lululemon, and 90+ more retailers.

🎯 SMART SIZE SCORING
• Brand-specific size adjustments (Nike runs small, H&M runs large, etc.)
• Choose your fit preference: Fitted, Regular, or Relaxed
• Supports Men's and Women's sizing
• Multiple measurement profiles (for partners, kids, etc.)
• Inches and CM support

👟 CLOTHING + SHOES
Enter your chest, waist, hips, inseam, and shoe size once. Size Oracle does the rest — showing your recommended size directly on product pages.

HOW IT WORKS
1. Click the extension icon and enter your measurements
2. Save your profile
3. Browse any supported store — your recommended size appears automatically

Built by Varun Meda — making online shopping stress-free, one size at a time.

## Permissions Justifications

### storage
Used to save user measurement profiles locally on the device.

### activeTab
Used to detect the current shopping site and display size recommendations on the active tab.

### scripting
Used to inject the size recommendation overlay onto supported shopping sites.

### host_permissions (<all_urls>)
Required to support 100+ shopping sites. Content scripts need to run on all matched retailer domains to detect product pages and display size recommendations.
