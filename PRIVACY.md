## Summary

Size-Oracle does **not** collect, transmit, or share any user data. Period.

## Data Storage

- Your body measurements (chest, waist, hips, inseam) and gender preference are stored **exclusively** in `chrome.storage.local` on your device.
- This data never leaves your browser.
- No data is sent to any server, API, or third party.

## Network Requests

Size-Oracle makes **zero** network requests. The extension operates entirely offline after installation.

## Analytics & Tracking

- No analytics
- No telemetry
- No cookies
- No fingerprinting
- No third-party scripts

## Permissions Explained

| Permission | Why |
|------------|-----|
| `storage` | Save your measurements locally |
| `activeTab` | Read size chart tables on the current page |
| `scripting` | Inject the size recommendation UI |

## Data Deletion

Click "Clear Data" in the extension popup to permanently delete all stored measurements. Alternatively, uninstalling the extension removes all data.
